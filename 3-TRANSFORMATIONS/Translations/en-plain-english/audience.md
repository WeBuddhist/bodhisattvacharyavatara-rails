# Audience Profile — Plain English Translation (en-plain-english)

This profile defines the "ideal reader" for the Plain English transformation track.

---

## 1. Demographics and region
- **Primary Region:** Global English speakers.
- **Age Range:** 13+ (General audience).
- **Education:** Middle school to high school reading level.
- **Background:** Secular or non-Buddhist background; people looking for an accessible entry point to the text.

## 2. Prior knowledge and reading level
- **Knowledge of Buddhism:** None to minimal. Unfamiliar with terms like "Emptiness", "Karma", or "Bodhisattva".
- **Reading Level:** Grade 8–10. Prefers clear, direct communication without academic or theological density.
- **Linguistic background:** Native or fluent English speakers who may find traditional scholarly translations opaque.

## 3. Use cases and reading settings
- **Primary Use:** Personal study, initial exposure to the *Bodhisattvacaryāvatāra*.
- **Setting:** Casual reading (on a phone, during a commute, or as part of a daily habit).
- **Goal:** To understand the core message and practical advice of the text without getting bogged down in philological or sectarian debates.

## 4. Motivations and pain points
- **Motivations:** Seeking ethical guidance, mental peace, or an introduction to Mahayana Buddhist thought.
- **Pain Points:** Traditional translations are too "heavy", use too many untranslated terms, or have overly complex sentence structures.
- **Desired Outcome:** A version of the text that "speaks directly" to them in modern, everyday language.
