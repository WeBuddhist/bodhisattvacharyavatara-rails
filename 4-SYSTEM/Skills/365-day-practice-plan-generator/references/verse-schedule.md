# 365-Day Bodhisattvacharyavatara Verse Schedule

This schedule distributes Śāntideva's Bodhisattvacharyavatara (914 verses across 10 chapters) over 365 days of study, beginning at Chapter 1, verse 1 — the opening homage and statement of purpose — and proceeding sequentially through all ten chapters.

The plan uses 2–3 verses per day, with slower pacing through the longer, denser chapters (Chapters 6, 8, 9).

## Notes for the skill

- **Flexibility**: The user may override any day's assignment by specifying chapter and verse range explicitly. The schedule is a guide, not a constraint.
- **Review days**: On reflection/review days, choose 2 verses from the chapter that the user (or the skill's judgment) identifies as most significant for re-contemplation. Generate a full 7-section plan as normal.
- **Final days 361–365**: These are integration days. Select one verse each from Chapters 1, 4, 6, 8, and 9 that feels most alive for the practitioner — the skill should ask the user which verse they'd like to revisit, or use its judgment to pick a famous/important verse from each chapter.
